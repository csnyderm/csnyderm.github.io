What is a Bachelor of Innovation?

A Bachelor of Innovation degree is an accredited degree created at the University of Colorado Colorado Springs. The degree plan focuses on preparing students to solve problems and to develop themselves and their businesses. Students work in cross-disciplinary teams made up of peers in degrees focused on business, engineering education, and more, to provide an impact to clients made up of local and national businesses.
